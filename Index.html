Premium haircare products delivering exceptional results

We combine years of expertise in the industry together with a passion for creating the best haircare products, that nourish, protect, and enhance the natural beauty of your hair.

Kusco-Murphy combines professional performance with thoughtful formulations, delivering high-quality haircare while remaining committed to sustainability and ethical practices.

With a reputation for salon quality haircare products to your home

Our brand uses botanical extracts, essential oils and plant-based proteins that are gentle on the hair and scalp while delivering effective results. Whether your hair needs hydration, volume, or everyday care, Kusco-Murphy offers solutions to suit your needs.

All things considered, we are a proudly Australian owned small business. Supplying locally manufactured hair products since 1995.

The most beautiful things in life are simple pleasures

While producing the Kusco-Murphy Hair Collection, simplicity was always a priority. Our Collections have been created with you in mind. Exotic combinations of essential oils , vitamins and minerals to gently cleanse, condition and prep your hair for the ultimate styling experience. SLES , SLS and Sulphate free for all our Washes & Rinses. Kusco-Murphy opposes animal testing.

Simply: The Kusco-Murphy experience embodies the essence of nature, Australian style.
